## Overview

**F99** is a conceptual framework designed for the compression, normalization, and formal representation of complex informational structures.
Its purpose is to transform rich, unstructured content into a compact, coherent, and reusable form while preserving its essential semantic structure.

## Purpose

The framework aims to:

- reduce informational complexity while preserving meaning  
- enable consistent and comparable representations of complex content  
- support reuse and reinterpretation across contexts  
- facilitate continuity of reasoning in constrained environments

- **Selection of relevant components**
